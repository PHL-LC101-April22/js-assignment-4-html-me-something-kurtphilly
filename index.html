Running, and more specifically trail running, has become an outlet to cope and relieve stress, explore new places, challenge my physical and mental abilities and create wonderful memories with friends, new and old. Over the last six years I have had the opportunity to run and take part in races that have become a powerful example of what we can accomplish if we apply ourselves and keep moving forward. I hope to continue my running career while visiting new places and meeting new people toward my destination.
